Pole

Connecting Questions With Answers

Ask anyone anything

With Pole, create surveys and get them answered by the people you care about. Whether you need dental students in Cincinatti or Harry Potter fans in Hong Kong, our survey platform gets you the anwswers you need, quickly and reliably.

Turn time into money

Have a few free minutes on the way home from work? Make at least $1 for every survey you take, and turn that free time into free cash. Pole is an opinion marketplace, and we reward our best users with the most lucrative surveys.

Building an opinion marketplace

Filter respondents by a growing list of fine-grained criteria, including real-time location, interests, and travel history.

Pole is optimized to reward users with the most extensive and complete profiles, incentivizing a growing, highly diverse community of respondents. We pass on most of the money you pay us to our respondents, and use several techniques to identify quality responses.

We crowdsource surveys through our mobile platform, allowing you to reach a wider cross-section of the populations you care about.
